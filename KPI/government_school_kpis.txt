
GOVERNMENT SCHOOL KPI DOCUMENTATION

ACADEMIC KPIs
1. Average Internal Assessment Score
Formula: AVG(marks_obtained / max_marks) * 100

2. Exam Pass Percentage
Formula: (Passed Students / Students Appeared) * 100

3. Subject-wise Performance Index
Weighted average of subject marks across exams

4. Academic Growth Rate
Formula: Current Year Avg Score - Previous Year Avg Score


ATTENDANCE KPIs
5. Student Attendance Rate
Formula: (Present Days / Working Days) * 100

6. Chronic Absenteeism Rate
Definition: Students with attendance less than 75%

7. Teacher Attendance Compliance
Formula: (Teacher Present Days / Working Days) * 100


SPORTS KPIs
8. Sports Participation Rate
Formula: (Students participating in at least one sport / Total students) * 100

9. Sports Achievement Score
District Level = 2 points
State Level = 4 points
National Level = 6 points


SOCIAL & CO-CURRICULAR KPIs
10. Co-Curricular Engagement Index
Formula: (Students participating in activities / Total students)

11. Leadership Participation Rate
Formula: (Students with leadership roles / Total activity participants)


INFRASTRUCTURE KPIs
12. Facility Availability Index
Formula: Available facilities / Required facilities

13. School Readiness Index
Composite of infrastructure, teacher availability, and attendance


COMPOSITE SCHOOL PERFORMANCE INDEX (SPI)
SPI = 
0.35 * Academic Score +
0.25 * Attendance Score +
0.15 * Sports Score +
0.15 * Activity Score +
0.10 * Infrastructure Score
